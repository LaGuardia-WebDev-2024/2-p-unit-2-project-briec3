Darth Vader

Darth Vader is the main antagonist in the original Star Wars trilogy. He was once a Jedi Knight named Anakin Skywalker, but he turned to the dark side of the Force and became a Sith Lord. Darth Vader is known for his iconic black armor, deep voice, and mastery of the Force.
